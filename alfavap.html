Alfavap PHE

Engineered for liquid concentration and evaporation duties. Rising-film plate technology for high heat transfer and superior product quality.

Rising Film Efficiency.

Alfavap is the specialized plate heat exchanger for evaporation duties. Utilizing a rising-film principle, it provides extremely high turbulence and heat transfer coefficients even in low-temperature difference evaporation systems.

Its compact design and short residence time make it ideal for heat-sensitive products, including sugar juice, starch, and fruit juices—ensuring the highest product quality with minimum energy consumption.
