Our Story

Brigantine Engineering was established with a clear purpose: to bring the world's best thermal and separation technology to Australian industry, backed by the kind of hands-on technical support that truly makes a difference.

From our base in Matraville, New South Wales, we support customers across every state and territory — working closely with manufacturers, miners, food producers, marine operators and facility managers to optimise their processes and reduce their operating costs.

What We Do

Product Supply

We source and supply premium heat exchangers, separators and decanters from world-leading manufacturers — selecting the optimal equipment for your specific process and operational requirements.

Application Engineering

Our engineers help you select, size and configure the right equipment for your duty. We consider process conditions, fouling potential, energy targets and total lifecycle cost to recommend the best fit.

Energy Optimisation

Through our Energy Hunter programme, we identify real, measurable opportunities to reduce electricity and fuel consumption in your facility — delivering direct, provable cost savings.

After-Sales Support

We provide responsive after-sales support including technical advice, spare parts supply and maintenance guidance — ensuring your equipment continues to perform at its best.

Commissioning Assistance

Our team can assist with on-site commissioning and installation guidance, ensuring your new equipment is set up correctly and operating at full efficiency from day one.

Technical Training

We provide application and product training for your engineering and operations teams — building your team's capability to get the most out of your thermal and separation equipment.
