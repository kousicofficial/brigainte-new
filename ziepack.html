Alfa Laval Ziepack

Compact welded in-column condensers and reboilers for demanding refinery and petrochemical processes.

Extreme Process Integration

Ziepack is an innovative plate heat exchanger solution that integrates directly into column heads or shells. Utilizing advanced laser welding and hydroforming technology, it provides high heat transfer performance while minimizing installation space and pressure drops.

Ideal for boiling and condensing duties, it reduces mechanical stress and infrastructure requirements, leading to significant cost savings in new builds and retrofits.
