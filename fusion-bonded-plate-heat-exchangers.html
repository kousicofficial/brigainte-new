Total Corrosion Resistance.

The fusion-bonded heat exchanger range represents the ultimate in modern brazed-plate technology. Unlike traditional units which use copper or nickel as a filler, these are constructed from 100% high-grade stainless steel using a patented fusion process.

This results in a unit with the compactness of a brazed exchanger but with the chemical compatibility and purity of a fully welded high-end heat exchanger. Perfect for ultrapure water, corrosive refrigeration duties, and aggressive process fluids.
