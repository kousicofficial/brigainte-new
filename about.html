Who We Are

Brigantine Engineering Services Australia is a specialised provider of industrial heat exchange and separation equipment. We partner with world-leading technology brands to deliver solutions that Australian industry relies on for energy efficiency, process quality and operational uptime.

Based in Matraville, NSW, we serve customers across the country — from food and beverage manufacturers to mining operations, marine fleets and industrial facilities of all kinds.
