Our Core Industries

Pasteurisation, CIP, brewing, dairy processing, juice production and vegetable oil refining — we deliver hygienic, certified thermal and separation equipment designed to meet the strictest food safety regulations.

Generator cooling, hydraulic system temperature control, mineral separation and tailings management — our robust equipment handles the demanding conditions of Australian mining operations reliably and efficiently.

🚢 Marine & Offshore

Heavy fuel oil conditioning, lube oil purification, freshwater generation and jacket water cooling — we supply specialised equipment trusted by marine operators for its compact design and reliability at sea.

🏗️ HVAC & Building Services

Free-cooling systems, chiller protection, district heating substations and domestic hot water — our heat exchange solutions drive significant energy savings in commercial and industrial buildings.

🧪 Chemical & Petrochemical

High-pressure, corrosion-resistant heat exchangers and separators for chemical processing, oil refining, catalyst recovery and solvent applications — with wide material selection including titanium and Hastelloy.

💧 Water & Wastewater

Sludge dewatering, RO pre-treatment and heat recovery from wastewater — our decanter centrifuges and wide-gap plate heat exchangers deliver efficient results for water utilities and industrial effluent treatment.
