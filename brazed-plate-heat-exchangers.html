Product Overview

The CB line of copper-brazed plate heat exchangers combines precision with a remarkably compact footprint, designed specifically for tight installations without compromising on thermal impact.

Utilizing advanced copper-brazing technology, these units are hermetically sealed under high temperature, ensuring excellent thermal conductivity, immense durability, and long-term energy savings.
