Heat Exchange Solutions

Gasketed Plate Heat Exchangers

The most flexible and serviceable type of plate heat exchanger. Available in multiple configurations — from standard industrial to wide-gap and semi-welded — for virtually any thermal duty.

Brazed Plate Heat Exchangers

Compact, hermetically sealed copper-brazed units delivering exceptional thermal performance and durability for refrigeration, HVAC and high-pressure applications.

Fusion Bonded Heat Exchangers

All-stainless construction without traditional brazing materials — ideal for applications requiring high-purity, corrosion resistance and compatibility with aggressive fluids.

Evaporators & Condensers

Specialised Alfacond and Alfavap units for steam condensation and evaporation duties — delivering compact, efficient thermal performance in vacuum and steam applications.

Separation Solutions

High-Speed Disk Stack Separator

Centrifugal separation of liquids and fine solids — used widely in marine fuel and lube oil conditioning, dairy, food processing and chemical applications.

Decanter

Continuous solid-liquid separation for sludge dewatering, tailings management, oil recovery and chemical processing. Robust construction for demanding industrial environments.

Liquid-Liquid Separator

Efficient separation of two immiscible liquids (such as oil and water) using centrifugal force — delivering cleaner outputs and better recovery than traditional settling methods.
