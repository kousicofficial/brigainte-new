Restore Peak Efficiency

Fouling, scaling, and aging gaskets are the primary causes of reduced efficiency and leaks in gasketed plate heat exchangers (GPHE). Brigantine Engineering offers expert servicing to restore your equipment's thermal performance and ensure operational reliability.

As specialists in Alfa Laval GPHE technology, we follow strict manufacturer procedures for cleaning, crack detection, and regasketing for all industrial and sanitary lines.

Specialized Services

Chemical Cleaning (CIP)

Safe and effective removal of fouling without dismantling the plate pack, restoring heat transfer and reducing power consumption.

Precision Regasketing

Highest quality gasket installation with specialized adhesives and alignment tools for a perfectly sealed plate pack.

Crack Detection

Advanced dye penetrant testing to identify microscopic cracks and pinholes in plates before they lead to cross-contamination.

Full Refurbishment Scope

  • Plate pack disassembly and inspection
  • High-pressure water cleaning
  • Chemical bath treatment
  • Mechanical frame repair and painting
  • Hydrostatic pressure testing after assembly
