Alfacond PHE

Specifically engineered for vacuum condensation and steam duties. A compact, cost-effective alternative to bulky shell-and-tube condensers.

Precise Steam Duty Profile.

Alfacond is the world's first plate heat exchanger designed specifically for vacuum condensation. Featuring large vapor inlets and an asymmetrical plate pattern, it delivers incredibly low pressure drops for steam and vapor, while managing cooling water with peak efficiency.

Used extensively in evaporation systems, turbine exhaust, and chemical condensation loops, Alfacond provides a fraction of the mass and footprint of legacy technology, with much higher heat transfer coefficients.
